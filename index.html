Welcome to Terminal Installer: Xposed

The story: Why I created Terminal Installer: Xposed

Ever since I rooted my first android device & stumbled upon xposed installer....I FELL in love with technology all over again...Yet after lollipop & even marshmallow came out...it was getting harder & harder to flash my newer devices with the xposed framework until every DAMN device that I bought either didn't have a custom recovery nor could one flash it!

HOWEVER in the midst of all that HELL...I finally found an alterative & even a safe way to install the xposed framework!

Terminal Installer was borned!

This tool can & WILL install the latest version of the xposed framework on any compatible device without a recovery if sporting Lollipop+ & either have "arm," "arm64," or "x86" as its ABI.

Incompatible devices list:

  1. All SAMSUNG devices
  2. Non-arm or non-arm64 or non-x86 devices
  3. a certain Dell Venue 8 7840(probably more) 4.some stock Sony roms...however ALL Sony devices will be blocked from flashing xposed until the fatal bootloop is fixed!

Side Note(s)

Isn't it Obvious how my tool works? All it does is execute a script in a terminal & that's ALL...nothing special whats so ever, however MANY safety protocals are put in place to make sure the right package is downloaded & finally flashed!

In order for my app to function...

I modified all xposed framework packages a bit...No big changes....so updating packages manually for me won't take long to push out continuous updates of Terminal Installer: Xposed.
