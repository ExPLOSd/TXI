Welcome to Terminal Installer: Xposed

The story: Life without Xposed!

Ever since I rooted my first android device & stumbled upon xposed installer....I FELL in love with technology all over again...Yet years later when lollipop & even marshmallow came out...it was getting harder & harder to flash my newer devices with the xposed framework until every DAMN device that I bought either didn't have a custom recovery nor could one flash it!

However in the midst of all that HELL...I finally found an alterative & even a safe way to flash the xposed framework for devices that are Lollipop+ & do NOT have a custom recovery!

Terminal Installer was borned!

This tool can & WILL install the latest version of the xposed framework on any compatible device without a recovery if sporting Lollipop+ & either have "arm," "arm64," or "x86" as its ABI.

Side Note(s)

Isn't it Obvious how my tool works? All it does is execute a script in a terminal & that's ALL...nothing special whats so ever, however MANY safety protocals are put in place to make sure the right package is downloaded & finally flashed!

In order for my app to function properly & be responsive...

I had to modify all xposed framework packages a bit...No big changes....so updating packages manually for me won't take long to push out continous updates of Terminal Installer

What I learn from all this?

Even though I had to throw away all the experiences that I had in developing android apps out the window & start fresh because of all the changes to material design guildlines & code optimization...however I did learn java all over again & utilize many new libaries in my app that I will disclose soon...In all honestly it was HELL, but it was fun.
